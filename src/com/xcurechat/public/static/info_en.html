Welcome to Tainichok, a completely free website with no moderators and advertisements!

If you are underaged or can be offended by an unmoderated talk or posted images, please leave now!

If you stay, we are glad to tell you that Tainichok can offer you plenty of wonderful things!

For instance, you can share images (jpg, png, ...), music (mp3) and videos (mp4, flv, ...) using your chat and forum messages.

Use more than a two hundred (200+) of various smiles!

Transliterate text, i.e. to convert from Latin to Russian characters, almost in every field and dialog window!

Take advantage of message flows that allow for a better visualization of chat conversations!

Set up your profile: upload photos, avatars and do many other things.

Find people by using extended user search, e.g. search by profile images, description, name and etc.

Create public and private chat rooms, and manage users in the latter ones.

Send public and private chat messages as well as offline and forum messages to users who are offline.

We do not have any moderators because each of us is a moderator for his own!

You can hide message content by clicking on the message title, and block messages from users!

If you wish, you can always delete your profile, but please stay at least until you experience all the wonderful things we have to offer!
